Games

GOL

Yet another Game of Life remake.

This implementation doesn't have infinite boundaries for cells. Any cell that has neighbors that are out of the grid count as alive. Try loading some of the prefabs to see some cool patterns.

Amaze

A maze with a mind-bending twist.

Collect the colored balls and place them back into the right buckets.

Play in "Fun" mode, or be the first to win in "Not Fun" mode! Race against a friend by choosing the same maze number and writing down your finish time.

Sweeper

A Minesweeper remake.

Click on squares to reveal what is beneath. But be careful, or you may just click on a bomb! Use the numbers as hints to where the bombs are and mark all bombs by right clicking on the squares. If you reveal all bomb-free squares, you win!
